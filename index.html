Gau Raksha

Preservation and research centre for indigenous Cows

Our centre for the research of indigenous cows have been honoured as the best national and state-level breeding center and has received various other awards. The center stretches in an area of more than 25-acre land.

  • We were faced with the challenge of farmers preferring the Jersey cows over the indigenous cows for one simple reason that is the production of the latter being less.
  • To improve the yield of the indigenous cows, state-of-the-art research center was developed.
  • Byproduct of such as cow dung was used to promote Grah Udhyog created employment for the women’s in the production of diyas (lamps), dhoop, Gau Mutra ark, Gau Kashth etc facility inside our research centre.
  • In the chain of above initiatives, 1.25 lakh diyas were sent to Ayodhya on the occasion of Deepotsav.
